## Project Description

MeetMode is an AI-powered networking platform that helps people discover and connect with nearby attendees who are open to meaningful conversations during hackathons, conferences, meetups, coworking spaces, and networking events.

Traditional networking often relies on chance interactions, making it difficult to identify people with similar interests or determine who is open to connecting. MeetMode solves this by allowing users to join an event, enable Meet Mode, discover compatible attendees, receive AI-powered match recommendations, and communicate through an integrated chat system.

The platform focuses on privacy, consent-first networking, and intelligent recommendations powered by AI.

---

## Approach

We designed MeetMode as a real-time networking platform with a simple and intuitive user flow.

Users create an account, build their professional profile, join an event, and choose whether to enable Meet Mode. Once enabled, they become discoverable by other attendees in the same event.

The backend manages authentication, profile management, events, chat, notifications, and matching logic, while Valkey is planned for high-performance real-time data storage and Breeth AI for long-term memory and intelligent recommendations.

The project demonstrates how AI can enhance networking by recommending meaningful connections based on skills, interests, and networking goals.
